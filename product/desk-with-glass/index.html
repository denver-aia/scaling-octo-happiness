Description

KPDI offers a wide range of architectural glass options — reflective, tinted, and coated (low-e) — meeting both aesthetic and performance needs for modern buildings. sustainable & energy-efficient design in line with our commitment to sustainability, kpdi promotes the use of low-e glass in all projects. these highperformance coatings help reduce heat gain, lower energy use, and improve overall occupant comfort — an ideal choice for the middle east climate.

Key Benefits :
Thermal Control
Reduces solar heat entry and keeps interiors cooler
Energy Efficiency
Lowers cooling load and electricity consumption
UV Protection
Blocks harmful rays and prevents fading of furnishings.
Visual Comfort
Allows natural daylight while minimizing glare.
Sustainability
Supports green-building standards and eco-friendly design.

Available brands : Saint-Gobain | Guardian | Emirates glass | Agc obeikan

All glass options are available in single, double, or insulated units, as well as
laminated glasses customized for façades, windows, skylights, and curtain wall.
